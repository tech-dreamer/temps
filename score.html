Score FAQ

  • Daily Score Earn 🪙 for each High & Low forecast based on accuracy compared to actual temps
  • Green (🟢) Perfect or awesome forecast (error of 0-1 degrees)
  • Yellow (🟡) Good or mid forecast (error of 2-3)
  • Red (🔴) Poor or bad forecast (error of ≥ 4)
  • Coins (🪙) Earn 10🪙 for 0 error, 7🪙 for 1 error, 4🪙 for 2 error, 1🪙 for 3 error, 0🪙 for ≥ 4 error
  • Mood (💗) Earn +2💗 for 0 error, +1💗 for 1 error, but lose -1💗 for ≥ 4 error
  • Share copies the score summary for you to easily share on social media or anywhere
  • Hourly Score Earn 2🪙 for 0 error, 1🪙 for 1 error
  • Is my score good? What is a good score?
    Generally, a score of 8🪙 in a city is considered good. A total daily score of 20🪙 is great, and a score of 30🪙 is awesome.
    Making more forecasts across more cities should boost your total 🪙 earned, helping you rise to the top of the leaderboard.

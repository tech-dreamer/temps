My Daily Forecasts

How to Play

  • Choose Today or Tomorrow from the forecast day selector.
  • Enter your forecasts for today's or tomorrow's High and Low before the cutoff times.
  • Feel free to use NWS, AccuWeather, Weather Channel, WU, or LLMs to help guide your forecasts.
  • Click Save to save your forecasts. You can edit forecasts until the cutoff times for each city.
  • Click View My Score the next morning to see results verified by NWS/WU and your shareable score. Good luck!
  • Repeat with Hourly Forecasts, then see your results each hour and all of today's hourlies at 8 PM ET. Have fun 😊
ⓘ Dates shown in Pacific Time (PT)

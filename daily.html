My Daily Forecasts

How To Solve the Puzzle

  • Choose Today or Tomorrow from the forecast day selector.
  • Enter your forecast for today’s or tomorrow’s High and Low before the cutoff time.
  • Click Save to save your forecasts. You can edit forecasts until the cutoff time.
  • Your prior forecasts (if any) appear as My current forecast.
  • Click View My Score the next morning to see your results. Good luck!
ⓘ Dates shown in Pacific Time (PT)
